About us...

We are a team of 3 young coders wanting to share our passion with you and encourage you to take your first steps towards coding. In order to do that, we decided to create a website where you could explore the world of coding and learn how to create your own website.

We started off as 3 inexperienced teenagers who decided to participate in a programme for young girls organised by Vodafone Greece, named #CodeLikeAGirl. After attending hours of classes and learning from a group of experienced developers and designers, we acquired the skills needed to create our very own website

This website is supposed to help you create a website, using coding languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript. We provide information for these 3 languages, the latest discoveries in the field, events for expanding your knowledge on coding, as well as links to other websites that could be used as manuals for your own technological accomplishments.

Overall, we provide everything that helped us start our journey and we hope it will be as useful and fun for you as it was for us.
